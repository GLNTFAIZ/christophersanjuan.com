ABOUT ME

CREATIVE DIRECTOR / EDITOR

My name is Christopher San Juan, better known as GLMR. I am a media-based creative director and editor who works within the entertainment industry. I primarily work within the fields of video, picture, and design including both static and motion. My mission is to bring vibrant and visceral styles with aspects of whimsy to the forefront of audiences through the delivery of stylized medias through the use of vintage equipment and techniques.
